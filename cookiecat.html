Cookie Cat

Cookie Cat from Steven Universe

Ingredients

  • 160 g flour
  • 80 g cocoa powder
  • 75 g butter, soft
  • 50 g light brown sugar
  • 100 g white sugar
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp vanilla
  • 110 g milk

Steps

  1. Combine flour and cocoa powder in a small bowl.
  2. Sift the mixture onto a piece of parchment paper.
  3. In the bowl of a stand mixer, cream together the butter, sugar, baking soda and salt for 5 minutes until the mixture is well combined and fluffy.
  4. ombine the vanilla and milk in a small bowl.
  5. With the mixer on low speed, tip 1/3 of the dry ingredients, then half of the milk.
  6. Scrape down the sides of the bowl, then add another 1/3 of the dry ingredients and the remaining milk and the vanilla.
  7. Roll the dough out to 4 mm thickness between two pieces of parchment.
  8. Remove the top layer of parchment and cut out the cookie design using a cutter.
  9. Transfer the cookies to a large rimmed baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  10. Bake at 350 (or 176 Celsius for real world measures) for 7-10 minutes, just until the cookies look dry.
  11. Allow the cookies to cool on the sheet tray until completely cool.
