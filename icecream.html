Sea Salt Ice Cream

Sea Salt Ice Cream from Kingdom Hearts

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup milk
  • 1 ½ cup cream
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • Teal gel-based food coloring

Steps

  1. Mix together 1 ½ cups each of high quality milk and cream.
  2. Combine 3 tbsp cornstarch in about 1 cup of milk and cream mixture until it is dissolved and no lumps remain.
  3. Bring the remaining milk and cream mixture close to a simmer on the stovetop.
  4. Then, ¾ cup sugar is added directly to the milk and cream on the stovetop until it dissolves over medium-low heat until just simmering.
  5. At this point add the cornstarch mixture until combined. Let that all cook for about 1 minute.
  6. Add one heaping tsp of sea salt to custard on stovetop as it finishes thickening.
  7. Strain into a heat-proof bowl. Add some gel-based teal food coloring.
  8. Into an ice cream machine it goes for about 25 minutes.
  9. After that’s done, pour into a piping bag in order to cleanly fill popsicle molds.
  10. Use stick alignment to assure sticks stand up straight, and in the freezer it goes for at least 6 hours up to overnight.
  11. Run under hot water to easily take out popsicles and enjoy!
