Krabby Patty

Krabby Patty from Spongebob

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chuck steak
  • 1 pound bone-in short ribs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp MSG
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 ounces shredded lettuce
  • 1 slice yellow American cheese
  • 1 small onion, sliced thinly
  • 1 tomato, sliced thinly
  • Ketchup to taste
  • Mustard to taste
  • 1 large dill pickle, sliced thinly
  • 4 sesame seed hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Trim and cut beef into 1-inch pieces and spread evenly on a parchment-lined baking sheet
  2. Place in the freezer for 15 minutes, along with the blade of a food processor, until beef is firm
  3. Pulse in the food processor until beef is ground and pebbly
  4. Shape into 4 patties, and season with salt, pepper, and MSG
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat until barely smoking
  6. Add patties and reduce heat to medium - cook to desired doneness
  7. On the burger buns, assemble the burgers in this order: patty, lettuce, cheese, onion, tomato, ketchup, mustard, pickles, top bun
